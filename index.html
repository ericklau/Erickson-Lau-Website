About Me

"Life begins at the end of your comfort zone". Which is why I left my comfort zone, Hawaii and headed to the unknown, Virginia which is where life really began. From the sandy beaches of Hawaii to the rocky mountains of Virginia I had no idea how my life would change. Coming to Virginia I knew no one however as I stepped out of my shell I began to do things I've never thought of doing before such as eating grits, playing cornhole, and jamming to country music. These activites were very unique experiences, and I loved every minute of it. As a sophmore at Virginia tech I plan to double major in Business Information Technology & Finance. My passion lies in managing people, technology, and analytics. With that in mind I am interested in a internship this summer involving business and/or technology.
